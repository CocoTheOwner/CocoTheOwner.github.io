OUR VISION

Since the rise of social networks, the need for effective data visualizations has paved its way.
Although many of these visualizations are useful in their own way and available for stand-alone download on the web, each of them has a certain weakness to them that should be kept in mind.
This is where we thought that it is time for a change.

As a result we created an easy-to-use application combining two effective visualization techniques to compensate for each of the weaknesses. By using edge-bundling to find patterns in data (in the form of a chord diagram), and a supporting alluvial diagram and bar for visualizing change in networks, we obtain the best of two worlds.
By providing the tool on the web instead of in a seperate computer program, advanced data visualization then becomes available to everyone. We aim to provide people with an easy-to-use, free visualization tool which they can use to go beyond simple graphs to explore their data.
