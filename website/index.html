OUR VISION

As students at Eindhoven University of Technology we understand the importance of data visualizations like no other. However as we have experienced over the years, a lot of data visualization tools are either inefficient for larger datasets or have to be installed seperately on your computer. This is where we thought; it is time for a change. By providing our visualization tool on the web instead of in a seperate computer program, advanced data visualization becomes available to everyone. We aim to provide people with an easy-to-use, and most importantly free visualization tool which they can use to go beyond simple graphs to explore their data.

OUR VISUALIZATIONS

Since we are still working hard on our website, currently the only visualization available is a primitive version of one of the visualizations that will eventually be implemented: an hierarchical edge bundle. This type of visualization is especially good to remove clutter and discover underlying patterns between clusters or hierarchical elements. The visualization as it is now has a seperate color for each cluster on the circle. The lines crossing the circle represent a connection between two nodes from different clusters. The thickness of the line indicates how often a connection between the two nodes has occurred over time. In later releases we plan to make the visualization more readable and interactive as described in our interim report.
